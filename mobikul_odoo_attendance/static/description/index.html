Now Manage Employee Attendances in Odoo Mobile App

Odoo Attendance Mobile App allows your employees to mark their attendance directly through their mobile phone. It also lets the employees check the sign in and sign out time; so that working hours can be checked as well as managed.

Your employees are the biggest asset of your organization; hence, it is important to maintain accurate information about their working hours. The app allows you to get the details like sign in and signout time of your employees so that you are aware of your employee timings.


Mobile App to Mark Employee Attendance in Odoo

The Odoo Attendance Mobile App lets the company employees mark their attendance with ease. The employee just needs to login to the mobile app via their Odoo ERP credentials.

After logging in, employees will be directed to the page where they can easily mark their attendance.

At the end of the day, while wrapping up work; the employees can checkout just with a click.


Perks of Attendance in Odoo Mobile App

  • Offers ease to manage each & every field forces employee attendance.

  • Eliminates the use of paperwork needed for attendance marking and monitoring.

  • Get live field employee attendance details.

  • No need for a heavy system to mark the attendance.
